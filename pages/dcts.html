Working at a regional authority, I am able to assist agencies within our region. I particulary enjoy this because I get to see how other agencies operate. Part of my work includes building a GTFS for the agency which allows me to learn the ins and outs of their transit system. I also get to create system maps for them like this one I made for Davidson County Transportation System (DCTS). I maintain an interactive web map that is accessible from DCTS' website.
