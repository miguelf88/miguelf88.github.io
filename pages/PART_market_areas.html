For a transit agency, a market area represents the geographic location that could access their service. This project required me to delineate the market areas for PART. A Park & Ride survey completed in mid-2018 helped me accomplish this. Using a free geocoder from Texas A&M University, I mapped the home location of PART's passengers who completed the survey. I then calculated the Euclidean distance from each home to the corresponding park & ride lot. Using the interquartile range method, I removed outliers from the dataset.

I located tables from the 2013-2017 5-year estimates of the American Community Survey using American Fact Finder. The tables were joined to a block group level geography from TIGER/Line shapefiles using the GEOID as the field to join on.
