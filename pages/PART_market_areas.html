--- title: PART Market Areas subtitle: Using Operations Dashboard for Transit Planning layout: "page" icon: order: hide: true ---

For a transit agency, a market area represents the geographic location that could access their service. This project required me to delineate the market areas for PART. A Park & Ride survey completed in mid-2018 helped me accomplish this. Using a free geocoder from Texas A&M University, I mapped the home location of PART's passengers who filled out the survey. I then calculated the Euclidean distance from each home to the corresponding park & ride lot. I then removed outliers using the interquartile range method.
