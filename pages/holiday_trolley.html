PART received a request to plan a route for the Downtown Greensboro organization. They were wanting to operate a holiday trolley during the Christmas season. PART received some general guidelines from Downtown Greensboro and a route was drawn in Remix . From Remix, I exported the geometry to a shapefile and brought it into ArcGIS Pro. I drew the candy cane symbol in Adobe Illustrator, saved it as an SVG file, and loaded it into my GIS as a custom symbol. Using Arcade, Esri's expression language, I wrote two functions to select stops along each side of the route and display them differently.
